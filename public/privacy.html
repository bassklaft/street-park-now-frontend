Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Street Park Now ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and website.

Information We Collect

  • Name and Email Address — collected when you create an account
  • Precise Location — collected when you use the live parking heat map or "Use my current location" feature, to show nearby street parking information
  • User ID — an internal account identifier assigned when you register
  • Product Interaction — searches performed and features used within the app, to manage your usage tier and improve the service

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide street parking schedules and heat map information near your location
  • To manage your account and subscription
  • To track usage against your plan limits
  • To send parking alerts via SMS if you opt in

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes. Payment processing is handled by Stripe — we never store your payment card information.

Location Data

Location is used solely to show nearby parking information. We do not store your location history or share it with third parties.

Data Retention

Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@streetparknow.app or visit streetparknow.vercel.app
